The Ranch House

The Ranch House is a building located at the back of The Ranch near the Plort Market, overlooking The Slime Sea. The door is interactable, and pressing the interact key (normally "E", "square" on PS4, and "X" on Xbox), takes you inside the house. While the player cannot explore as it is essentially just a menu, there is a range of options they can do - some of which are necessary for game progression.

Sleep Until Morning

Fast forwards to 6:00 am the next morning, regardless of how much or how little time, until 6 am, is left. All time-based actions such as slime feeding, plort production or garden growth will advance with the time that gets skipped.

Starmail

Starmail is a feature in the game that allows NPCs to contact Beatrix. Starmail is an in-game mechanic found through the Ranch House menu by clicking the "Starmail" button. It allows the player to read any Starmail the player receives over the course of the game. Any unread Starmail is automatically considered read once opening it. Any unread Starmail is automatically considered read once opening it. You will recieve starmail from Hobson 6 hours after reading Hobson's last note in The Glass Desert. This triggers the End Credits when you Return to Ranch. Although the story mode has been complete, you can still continue with the game.
