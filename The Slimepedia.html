Pink Slime

Pink Slimes are the most common slimes found in Slime Rancher and are among the first you will encounter. They are easily identified by their pink coloration and can spawn in practically any location outside of The Ranch. The pink slime will eat anything you feed it.

Phosphor Slime

The Phosphor Slime is a nocturnal slime easily identified by its violet coloration, wings, and soft bright glow. It is one of the most common slimes found at night, but does not spawn during the day.Its favourite food is the cuberry.

Tabby Slime

Tabby Slimes, or Tabbies for short, are one of the most common slimes in Slime Rancher, along with the Rock Slime, both second only to the Pink Slime. They are easily identified by their resemblance to domesticated cats, as well as their behavior and dietary requirement of Meat. Their favourite food is the Stony Hen

Honey Slime

Honey Slimes are uncommon slimes that reside in The Moss Blanket. T hey can be easily identified by their golden-brown coloration, and the three-hexagon crest on their foreheads. They also love to eat mint mangos

Puddle Slime

Puddle Slimes are aquatic Slimes that can spawn and float in shallow pools of water. On The Ranch, they require a specific habitat to survive, but can also thrive in the natural pond at The Docks, the river at Mochi's Manor and the artificial pond at Viktor's Lab.

Hunter Slime

The Hunter Slime is a relatively rare Slime that inhabits The Moss Blanket and The Glass Desert. While the pure slimes themselves are docile and pose no threat to the player, their Largo variants are always in a feral state when created, and if tamed can turn feral again when sufficiently agitated. Hunter Slimes love to eat roostro

quantum Slime

Quantum Slimes are Slimes that are commonly found in The Ancient Ruins and by the Dervish fountain in The Glass Desert. They have the ability to teleport to the location of their ghosts when agitated. They love to eat the phase lemon.

Dervish Slime

Dervish Slimes are Slimes which have a ring surrounding their body which are capable of producing cyclones when agitated. They are found exclusively in The Glass Desert. While they can be found infrequently around the Glass Desert in an agitated state, snaring its Gordo through Gordo Snares, or bursting the wild Dervish Gordo, they will spawn consistently throughout The Glass Desert when certain oases have been restored. The dervish slime love to eat pickled pears.

Tangle Slime

Tangle Slimes are Slimes with flowers on their heads that are capable of utilizing vines to reach food and plorts a small distance away from them to feed themselves, and can produce pollen when agitated. It is encountered exclusively in The Glass Desert. While they can be found by snaring its Gordo through Gordo Snares, or bursting the wild Tangle Gordo, they will spawn consistently throughout The Glass Desert when certain oases have been restored. Its favourite food is the painted hen

Rocky Slime

The Rock Slime is one of the most common slimes in Slime Rancher, tied with the Tabby Slime, both second only to the Pink Slime. They can be found almost everywhere at any time of the day. They have rocky crowns which are able to damage Beatrix on contact and can roll up into a rocky ball and roll in a straight line. Their favourite food is the heart beet.

Rad Slime

The Rad Slime is a Slime found exclusively in The Indigo Quarry, and in The Wilds entirely as Saber Largos. Unlike other Harmful Slimes, they do not deal damage directly, but when exposed to their aura for long enough, you will start taking constant damage as long as they remain within the aura. \ Rad slime love the oca oca.

Boom Slime

Boom Slimes are slimes that occasionally explode. They can found most frequently in the first two unlockable zones; The Indigo Quarry and The Moss Blanket. They love to eat riar hen.

Crystal Slime

Crystal Slimes are slimes that create sharp spikes on the ground for a minute or until dispersed with water. They spawn in two specific locations in The Indigo Quarry. Odd onion is their favourite.

Fire Slime

Fire Slimes are primarily encountered during Solar Anomalies in The Glass Desert. They require a specific habitat to survive and can feed on any food from the fruit, veggie, or meat categories as long as it has been burned on the Incinerator and converted to ash. Because of their similarities, they are often considered to be a counterpart to the Puddle Slime.

Mosaic Slime

Mosaic Slimes are Slimes that have a glass-like shell that periodically creates fiery glints of light similar to the ones produced in solar anomalies. They are exclusively found in The Glass Desert. While they may be obtained through snaring its Gordo through Gordo Snares and bursting the wild Mosaic Gordo, While an extremely rare spawn within later parts of The Glass Desert, they will only spawn consistently throughout when certain oases have been restored. Mosaic Slimes love the silver parsnip

Saber Slime

Saber Slimes are carnivorous slimes introduced with The Wilds update. They are found exclusively as Largo Slimes. They are the fourth feline-based slime introduced, after the Tabby Slimes, Lucky Slimes, and Hunter Slimes.

Lucky Slime

Lucky Slimes are rare Slimes that have a low chance of appearing in specific locations around the Far, Far Range. It is one of four feline-based Slimes in the game, along with the Tabby Slime, Hunter Slime and the Saber Slime.

Gold Slime

Gold Slimes are a rare species of slime that produce the most valuable plorts found on the Far, Far Range.
